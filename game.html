Rules of the dice game

  1. Enter an number between 3 and 6 to play with that many dice. The game will start when you hit submit.
  2. Each die is then rolled, and a score for that round is calculated based on the table below.
  3. You can keep pressing play to start a new round. The total score for all the rounds will be kept up to date.
